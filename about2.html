About Us

From Humble Begining to a Market Leader

Established in the Year 2016 First Energy has grown up to become one of the top five solar energy companies in Sri Lanka within a relatively short period of 5 years. This success story is a result of the presence of high calibre professionals in the industry from top to bottom in its hierarchy, led by Eng. Prabath Wickramasinghe who has more than 20 years of experience in the Sri Lankan energy industry and Eng. Asanka Thennakon who is one of the leading experts in the Solar Energy sector in Sri Lanka with over 10 years of experience in that sector.

The main business areas of the company comprise design, supply & installation of Solar PV systems and providing operation & maintenance services for such systems, and also providing consultancy, project management and EPC services and for roof top solar PV and utility scale ground mounted solar power projects.

The company continues to thrive in the solar energy industry through the founding vision of providing a quality service with utmost professionalism.

Vision

To contribute and to become an integral part of sustainable energy initiative as a high quality and affordable service provider in solar photo-voltaic system leveraging the expertise & innovations

Mission

  • Creating sustainable business models to achieve lowest Levelized Cost of Electricity (LCoE) with durability.
  • Creating expertise and developing skills re-quired for solar photovoltaic system integra- tions in the part of Electrical, Mechanical & Civil Engineering.
  • Training and capacity building of employ- ees for providing quality and affordable ser- vices to the clients
  • Achieving financial sustainability by venturing in to different segments in solar industry such as EPC contracts, Investments, Design, Consulting and project management services both locally and globally.

Our Team

Leadership

Prabath Wickramasinghe – Managing Director
Eng. Prabath Wickramasinghe is Electrical Engineer by profession (Univ. of Peradeniya 1995-1999). Also holds Management Qualifications from Chartered Institute of Management Accountancy (CIMA) of UK (1993-1998) & qualified in Marketing from Chartered Institute of Marketing UK (1993-1998). Eng. Wickramasinghe has extensive experience in Development & Marketing renewable energy especially small hydro and wind power since year 2000. Eng. Wickramasinghe has served as the President of Small Hydro Power Developers Association from 2016 to 2020 and Also, he has served as a member in the National Committee of Economic Development in Sri Lanka during the period 2005-2010
Asanka Tennakoon – Chief Executive officer
Eng. Asanka Tennakoon is an Electrical Engineer by profession (Univ. of Peradeniya 1995-1999). He holds a master’s degree from UK on Information Systems and Post Graduate Diploma in Geographical Information Systems & Remote Sensing (GIS& RS). Eng. Asanka is among the most experienced Solar Energy Engineers in Sri Lanka since 2009. He was instrumental in first ever Net Metering System and also in first ever solar, wind diesel hybrid project in Sri Lanka. He has contributed immensely to the industry by training young Engineers & Technicians and in preparation of standards for Solar Industry. He was instrumental in conceptualizing “Clustered mini ground mounted solar power plant concept” to utilize rural medium voltage distribution transformer network to reverse feed solar electricity through sub urban and urban areas to the grid substations that was later adopted by Ceylon Electricity Board and also included in the government policies.
Sanka Dulanja Sri – General Manager
Sanka Liyanage is an Mechanical Engineer by profession (Institute of Technology University of Moratuwa ). Sanka has started his career in 2013 in renewable energy industry as an assistant engineer. Since then he has involved in implementing number of solar PV projects with a cumulative capacity more than 15MW. He was the project engineer for Eluvaithivu mini grid Project, which is Sri Lanka’s first solar, wind & Diesel-powered mini grid off the coast of Kytes, Jaffna which is one of the most challenging assignment he had carried out so far. As an young engineer he has gain vast experience in project management and technical optimizations in solar PV industry
